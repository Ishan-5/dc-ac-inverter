## Objective

To design a basic inverter circuit capable of converting DC power from a battery source into usable AC output.

## Components Used

- 12V Battery
- Transformer
- Switching Transistors / MOSFETs
- Oscillator Circuit
- Heat Sink
- Connecting Wires

## Working Principle

The inverter converts 12V DC into alternating current using a switching mechanism. The oscillator generates alternating signals that drive the switching components, which in turn energize the transformer to produce stepped-up AC voltage.
